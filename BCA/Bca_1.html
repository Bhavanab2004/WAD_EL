Christ University

Christ University, established in 1969, is a top-ranked private university in Bangalore known for academic excellence and holistic education. The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program offers a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical experience in computer science, software development, and IT management.

Unique Features

  • NAAC ‘A+’ accredited university with an international academic reputation.
  • Modern labs, coding clubs, and interdisciplinary learning approach.
  • Global internship and exchange opportunities with partner universities.
  • Strong industry connections ensuring real-world exposure.
