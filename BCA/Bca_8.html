BMS College of Commerce and Management - BCA

BMS College of Commerce and Management (BMSCCM), located in the heart of Bengaluru, is a premier institution offering a high-quality BCA program. The course focuses on building strong programming, analytical, and business communication skills to prepare students for the IT industry.

Unique Features

  • Modern computer labs with industry-standard software and hardware.
  • Regular hackathons, coding bootcamps, and inter-college competitions.
  • Focus on both technical and managerial skills for IT careers.
  • Excellent placement record with top companies in India and abroad.
