Alliance University

Alliance University, located in Bengaluru, is one of India’s premier private universities offering a globally recognized Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program. The university emphasizes leadership, innovation, and entrepreneurship to shape future global business leaders.

Unique Features

  • Recognized as one of the best private universities in South India for management studies.
  • Industry-aligned BBA curriculum with global business perspective.
  • Opportunity for international immersion programs and global internships.
  • State-of-the-art campus and excellent placement support.
