Mount Carmel College

Mount Carmel College is one of Bangalore’s premier institutions for women, known for its excellence in business education. The BBA program aims to shape future leaders through innovation, management insight, and ethical business values. It combines academic rigor with real-world application.

Unique Features

  • One of India’s leading autonomous colleges with a strong legacy of women’s education.
  • Industry-driven curriculum emphasizing entrepreneurship and leadership.
  • Regular industry visits, seminars, and business fests.
  • Personality development and leadership enhancement programs.
