Indian Institute of Science (IISc) - M.Tech

IISc Bangalore is India’s premier research institute, renowned globally for advanced engineering and technology education. The M.Tech program at IISc emphasizes research, innovation, and cutting-edge technology, preparing students for leadership in academia and industry.

Unique Features

  • World-class research and advanced laboratories.
  • Strong focus on innovation, entrepreneurship, and academic research.
  • Collaboration with top international universities and industry partners.
  • Highly qualified faculty with global recognition.
