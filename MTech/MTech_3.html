BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE) - M.Tech

BMS College of Engineering, one of the oldest and most reputed institutions in Bangalore, offers M.Tech programs with a strong emphasis on innovation, research, and practical exposure. The college is known for its excellent infrastructure, industry connections, and academic excellence.

Unique Features

  • Autonomous institution affiliated with VTU and accredited by NBA and NAAC (A++ Grade).
  • Strong research culture with well-equipped labs and research centers.
  • Collaborations with reputed industries and government organizations.
  • Highly qualified faculty with academic and industrial experience.
