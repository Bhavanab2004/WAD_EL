New Horizon College of Engineering (NHCE) - M.Tech

NHCE Bangalore offers advanced M.Tech programs with a focus on research, technical innovation, and industry-relevant skills. The college emphasizes hands-on learning, projects, and preparing students for careers in top tech companies.

Unique Features

  • Industry-oriented M.Tech curriculum with modern labs.
  • Collaboration with IT and core engineering companies for internships.
  • Focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Experienced faculty guiding students in advanced projects and research.
