Christ University

Established in 1969, Christ University (Deemed to be University) is one of Bangalore’s leading private institutions, renowned for its strong emphasis on holistic education, discipline, and academic excellence. The School of Business and Management (SBM) offers a globally recognized MBA program with innovative pedagogy, industry partnerships, and international collaborations.

Unique Features

  • Dual specialization MBA with global exposure and international exchange programs.
  • Collaboration with leading universities such as Virginia Commonwealth University (USA) and University of Applied Sciences (Germany).
  • Modern infrastructure with smart classrooms, auditoriums, and dedicated incubation centres.
  • Comprehensive placement and career guidance cell with top corporate tie-ups.
  • Vibrant campus life encouraging research, entrepreneurship, and cultural diversity.

MBA Program Details

Program Master of Business Administration (MBA)
Duration 2 Years (Full Time)
Specializations Marketing, Finance, HR, Business Analytics, International Business
Exams Accepted CAT, XAT, MAT, CMAT, GMAT, ATMA, or CUET (Christ University Entrance Test)
