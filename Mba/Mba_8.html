Kristu Jayanti College - MBA

Established in 1999 and affiliated with Bengaluru North University, Kristu Jayanti College is one of the top B-schools in Bangalore. The MBA program here focuses on developing managerial competence, leadership skills, and ethical business practices. It has received high ratings for academic excellence, global exposure, and placement outcomes.

Unique Features

  • AICTE-approved and NAAC 'A+' accredited autonomous institution.
  • Global collaborations with universities in the USA, UK, and Europe for exchange programs.
  • Strong emphasis on experiential learning, case studies, and industry exposure.
  • Dedicated placement cell connecting students with top recruiters.
  • Offers dual specialization in Finance, HR, Marketing, Business Analytics, and Operations.
