BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE)

Established in 1946, BMS College of Engineering is one of India’s oldest and most reputed private engineering institutions, located in the heart of Bangalore. The Department of Management Studies (MBA) at BMSCE was established in 1992 and is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The program focuses on developing analytical, managerial, and leadership skills in students aspiring for careers in management and entrepreneurship.

Unique Features

  • NBA-accredited and NAAC ‘A++’ grade autonomous institution under VTU.
  • Modern infrastructure with dedicated management block and digital learning spaces.
  • Strong industry-academia interaction through seminars, internships, and guest lectures.
  • Focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship with an in-house startup incubation center.
  • Active placement cell with 100+ reputed recruiters visiting annually.
