Dayananda Sagar Business School (DSBS)

Dayananda Sagar Business School, part of the renowned Dayananda Sagar Institutions in Bangalore, is a leading B-school offering an MBA program affiliated with Bangalore University and approved by AICTE. The institute focuses on developing leadership, innovation, and entrepreneurship through experiential learning and global exposure.

Unique Features

  • Modern curriculum designed in collaboration with industry experts.
  • Strong emphasis on innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership training.
  • Regular corporate interactions, workshops, and case study-based learning.
  • International collaborations for student exchange and global immersion programs.
  • Dedicated placement cell with top recruiters from finance, IT, and consulting sectors.
